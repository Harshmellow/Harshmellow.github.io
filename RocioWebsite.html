How I Meet:Rocio

Ever since I meet Rocio back in El Salvador , I knew her for the independent person she was. While at times loud and aggressive(--sometimes going as far to biting your hair--), but that's what set her apart from all the other slim shady's. After losing contact with Rocio for their elementary years(aside from the times, that they visited during Christmas or Hanukkah which was too short), Uncle Geoff decided it was time for the "girls" to move, for a better education. I remember my excitement when I heard the "girls" were gonna live in the US. For the first time in my life, I had someone my age who dealt with the same shit that I had to go though("Someone what").
